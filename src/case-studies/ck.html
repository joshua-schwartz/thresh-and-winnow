CK Contracting

We Can Dig It.

CK was in need of a new visual identity, a website, and a corporate video. Their competition lacked flair, so we knew that a bold look could really set them apart.

Voyage Goals

  • Create a new visual identity that immediately evokes the land/site development industry while modernizing and simplifying their existing logo.
  • Deliver branded collateral to simplify and dress up the mundane materials standard within the industry, giving customers a great first-glance brand experience.
  • Design and develop a website that will quickly showcase CK's core services and the projects they have pushed, pulled, and dragged some dirt for.
