Renuva Back & Pain Centers

We've Got Your Back.

Renuva wanted to increase awareness and expand their customer base with a new brand identity, high-quality TV and Radio commercials, a new website and targeted social media presence, and online and social media advertising.

Voyage Goals

  • Attract customers by crafting a high-quality, professional visual identity.
  • Develop an age-sensitive online strategy that would include news, blog posts, and SEM.
  • Deploy an offline data-driven marketing strategy that uses unique URLs, phantom 1-800 numbers, and print advertising QR codes to track marketing performance more accurately.
