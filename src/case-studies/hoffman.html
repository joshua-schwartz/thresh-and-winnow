Hoffman Kitchen & Bath

Marketing Renovation.

Hoffman Kitchen & Bath wanted it all, so that's what we gave them. We overhauled their logo, freshened up the design of their website, and produced a beautiful company profile video. We also coordinated the social media, online and offline marketing, and PR events and contests for their 4 locations across Arkansas and Oklahoma with a unified strategic plan and audience-targeted messaging.

Voyage Goals

  • Increase brand awareness in order to drive foot traffic in multiple Oklahoma and Arkansas showroom locations.
  • Expand social media and online advertising efforts to new platforms like Houzz as part of a targeted marketing strategy.
  • Use LEAN principles to refine target audience by gathering demographic data with Google Adwords test campaigns.
