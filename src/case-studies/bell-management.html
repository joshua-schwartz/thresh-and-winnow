Welcome Home.

The folks at Bell Management needed a brand update and a website that would make it easier for prospective renters to find a home that fits their needs.

Voyage Goals

  • Create a visual identity that differentiates the brand from the competition and supports their position as a manager of beautiful, high-end properties.
  • Design and develop a website that assists potential tenants by offering powerful search tools to improve lead conversion.
  • Craft UIX that gives users quick access to available properties within their area.
