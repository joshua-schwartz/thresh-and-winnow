Brand Identity : Strategy

A brand's visual identity is its unique overall look. It's used to create distinction from other brands and assert a recognizable presence wherever customers encounter it. Fonts, colors, and a brand mark (aka logo) are some of the building blocks of your brand identity.

Brand Strategy

Your brand is not your product, your logo, your website, or your name - it's the perception that all of these things evoke. It's an impression created by all of your customers' interactions with your organization, and what they've heard about you. Brand Strategy exists to proactively manage what people think and feel when it comes to your brand.
